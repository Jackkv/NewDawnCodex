## Storage

- **Lockdowns**: Individual items that can be secured in your house
- **Secures**: Containers (chests, crates, etc.) that can be secured

## Placement

House placement requires:

- Sufficient gold to purchase the house deed and place it
- A valid location with enough flat terrain
- No interference with other houses, roads, or dungeon entrances

## Ownership

- Each account can own 1 house
- With 3 accounts, you can have a maximum of 3 houses total
- Houses require regular maintenance to avoid decay
